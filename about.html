About me

As a native speaker of Italian and Turkish, and a B2-level English speaker, I bring a multicultural perspective and strong communication skills to every team I'm part of.

I'm looking for a place to grow. I believe that real learning happens through doing, and I'm ready to take on meaningful tasks, learn from experienced professionals, and contribute with energy, reliability, and fresh perspective. Every challenge is an opportunity, and I'm eager to start proving that.
